Welcome to Shirdi

Shirdi is a holy town in Maharashtra, India, where Shri Sai Baba lived and preached. Devotees from around the world come here to seek blessings and peace.

History of Sai Baba

Sai Baba of Shirdi was an Indian spiritual master revered by both Hindu and Muslim devotees. He taught love, forgiveness, helping others, charity, contentment, and devotion to God.
